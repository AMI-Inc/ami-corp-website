About Us

 

George A. Carlsgaard CEO

Aerospace & Marine International (AMI) has been providing weather services to the maritime industry since 1991.

AMI maintains two operations centers in the USA and UK. These two centers are fully networked and work together to deliver global 24 hour weather forecasting services.

Our staff of skilled meteorologists provides optimal solutions for our client’s operational challenges. With our world-wide experience and extensive global meteorological and oceanographic data we are confident that we can help you increase your operational efficiency and lower your costs.

Today’s technological world and increasing environmental and legal concerns require a higher level of meteorological and oceanographic support than was needed in the past. AMI leads the industry with its team of chartered and certified meteorologists, oceanographic and forensic experts and knowledgeable application specialists and engineers.

“Good enough” is not enough.

We invite you to discuss your specific requirements and look forward to providing solutions to enhance your operations.

Please Contact your AMI Representative for more information.
